Problem Statement

Battery Health Monitoring

Electric vehicle battery performance degrades over repeated charge and discharge cycles. Estimating State of Health helps users identify battery condition, plan maintenance, and improve operational reliability before severe degradation affects vehicle usage.

Objectives

  • Predict battery State of Health using operational battery parameters.
  • Provide a professional Flask interface for model inference.
  • Store prediction history for review, reporting, and analytics.
  • Visualize battery health trends through dashboard charts and summaries.

Dataset Information

Battery Feature Set

The cleaned dataset contains EV battery operating features such as cycle count, voltage, current, temperature, charge time, discharge time, internal resistance, capacity, ambient humidity, C-rate, and the target variable SOH.
